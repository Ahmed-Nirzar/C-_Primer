(a) `int* ip, i, &r = i;`     
      
1. `int* ip` : `int*` (pointer to `int`)     
2. `int i` : `int`      
3. `int &r = i` : `int&` (reference to `int`)     

(b) `int i, *ip = 0;`      
     
1. `int i` : `int`    
2. `int* ip = 0` : `int*` (pointer to `int`)    

(c) `int* ip, ip2;`     
     
1. `int* ip` : `int*` (pointer to `int`)     
2. `int ip2` : `int`
